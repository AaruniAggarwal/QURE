Frequently Asked Questions

QURE is an appointment scheduler to facilitate seeking confirming and fulfilling appointments for doctor consultations health checks and diagnostic services at any of the Apollo Hospitals across the group through the website

You may log in with the ID and password and manage your appointments. You can cancel or reschedule your appointment with the same or a different doctor depending on the availability of doctor slot on the chosen date.

E-mail ID is mandatory for booking through the website. If you do not have an E-mail ID you can give a family members ID. The purpose of providing an E-mail ID is for easy communication that may be required from time to time. It is always advisable to provide an E-mail ID. However for telephonic booking E-mail ID is not mandatory.

You do not need to provide your details again when you book an appointment the next time. With the login ID and password you can book another appointment for yourself or any other family member. But you have to fill the patient details if he/she is new to QURE.

Currently the online payment facility is not available. You have to pay your bill amount at the respective hospital. The online payment facility will be available soon.

Please approach the reception/ QURE help desk regarding your appointment. The respective staff will guide you to the consultant. We request you to reach the hospital 15 minutes prior to your appointment for completing the registration process. You have to pay registration fee in addition to the consultation fee.
